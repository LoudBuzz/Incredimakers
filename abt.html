What is this?

This site is a library of Incredibox mods that either couldn't make it into the original library or can't make it into other libraries.
This is made by fans for fans, of course.

How to add mods?

To add mods, you need to publish the description, icon, download link, media (such as teasers or previews) and status in the Mods channel of our Discord server. After publishing them, you will have to wait until the page creator puts the mod in the libraries.
